Kadali Routhu

Kadali Routhu is a name associated with a prominent Telugu author, lyricist, dialogue writer, and publisher named Kadali Satyanarayana.

Profile

    Kadali Routhu is known for her insightful perspectives on feminism and women's experiences, exploring the "grey areas" and untold stories through her writing.

    She is the author of three best-selling Telugu books:

  • "Letters to Love": A story exploring first love and enduring chemistry.
  • "Kadali Kathalu": A collection of stories giving voice to women who defy societal expectations.
  • "Chick Lit": A book that delves into the societal rules imposed on women and challenges conventional notions of "chick lit".
Awards and recognition
She is the recipient of the 'Parnasala Youth Excellence (PYE) Awards 2025' for her contributions to literature.
